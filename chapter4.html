Building strong customer relations certainly helps contribute to a company’s success, and in today’s customer-centric market, it’s a critical focus area. After all, over 90 percent of consumers are likely to spend more money with businesses that offer exceptional experience.





WHAT IS CUSTOMER RELATIONS?

Customer relations refers to the methods, strategies, and processes a company uses to improve the customer experience on a short- and long-term basis. It’s the sum of all customer interactions and experiences. This may differ depending on the size of your business and the nature of customers.

Customer relations encompasses a wide variety of tasks. Take a look at some specific activities so you know where to start:

- Analysing customer feedback through surveys and customer service interactions
- Setting marketing strategies
- Working with IT and technical teams to streamline customer interactions and decrease wait times
- Building and maintaining brand credibility
- Ensuring the customer service experience is consistent across customer touchpoints
- Proposing solutions to frequent issues, bottlenecks, and

CUSTOMER RELATIONS VS. CUSTOMER SERVICE:

Customer relations and customer service share overlapping principles and sound like interchangeable terms, but they’re not quite the same thing. Customer service is usually reactive (helps customers in real-time), while customer relations is primarily proactive (strives to improve the customer experience and solve problems before they happen).

CUSTOMER

SERVICE

The success in retail fuel station business is based upon successful performance of its entire team members which ranges from site manager to the janitorial staff.

Customer service points have been defined to give clear and simple guidance to our attendants on how to serve our customers. These have been developed to ensure our customer gets a welcoming experience and continues to choose Taj Gasoline as the preferred partner on the road.



CUSTOMER SERVICE POINTS :

DIESEL CUSTOMER :

Diesel Customer Service Attendant

Receive: Always stand facing the site entrance & wave customer to the island in standing posture with a smiling face as soon as they turn into the site. Welcome: Greet driver with Salam and raised hand at the side of his driving seat with a smiling face.

Inquire about refueling: Inquire about the product & required quantity.

Ensure Safety & Show zero-meter reading: Ensure Engine is Turned Off & Customer is not smoking or using mobile phone. Lift the nozzle & point towards dispenser screen to show zero-meter reading to the customer.

Clean Windscreen: Clean customer’s windscreen after asking them for permission.

Offer Dua e Safar if it is not hanging in the vehicle.

Offer Water: Offer customer (driver & passengers) clean drinking water (cold in summer months) by using a clean glass on a tray.

Gup Shup (Having Conversation): During refueling, engage customer by asking customer about their journey. If a frequent customer, try to remember their family details and ask about their health. Inform them about Taj Services, especially Taj Network and stations on their way, Helpline Services. Inform them about any promotional scheme that is running.

Show Final Meter: Once finished refueling, point towards dispenser screen and show final meter reading to customer.

Facilitate Payment: Guide customer towards cashier and facilitate in payment.

Saying Goodbye: Say good bye (Khuda Hafiz / Allah Hafiz) and wish them well on their ourney with a smiling face and raised hand. Invite again whenever passing this way.



Diesel Cashier

Welcoming: Greet customer with Salam with a smiling face & eye contact.

Customer Profile: Enter customer vehicle and contact information in software.
If customer is new, extend special welcome and thank them for choosing Taj.
Create customer profile with correct vehicle & contact details.

Process Payment: Process payment and give computer generated invoice to customer.

Say Goodbye: Say good bye (Khuda Hafiz / Allah Hafiz) and wish them well on their journey with a smiling face.

Send Transaction Message: Ensure that system has sent transaction message to customer’s mobile phone.

RETAILERS AND SITE MANAGER’S
FORECOURT TIME:

A Retail Fuel Station's forecourt is its beating centre. The main part of business is done in the forecourt. The outcome of the game depends on it. To keep an eye on the personnel and interact with customers, both the retailer and the manager must spend regular time at the forecourt. The manager must be on the forecourt for a minimum of two hours each shift (4 Hours Per Day).



The site manager must spend time on the forecourt during busiest customer service times to oversee customer service and interact with consumers. He must go to the forecourt during off-peak hours to interact with the shift personnel as well as to oversee housekeeping and operational standards.

Key things to activities during Forecourt Time:

1- Observe overall site operations to highlight any areas for improvement especially traffic management
2- Engage Customers to learn about
   - Their feedback on experience at the fuel station
   - Their journey and their needs during the Journey that Taj can help
   - Insights about market and business
   - Learnings about other fuel stations and their best practices
3- Demonstrate measurement
4- Engage Site Team to motivate and inspire
5- Coach Site Staff through role modeling activity in customer service
6- Review housekeeping and safety standards

TAJ EHSAS HELPLINE:

Taj Ehsas is a 24/7 free helpline that is accessible to clients. Travelers in need can dial "0311-1111825," and their requests are forwarded to the appropriate station. The station manager must reply to requests immediately and provide all necessary assistance.

In order to advertise the service, a sticker is attached to each of the canopy column that houses the station where the dispenser is located and facing the canopy's exit. If there is just one column at the station, the sticker needs to be applied to both sides of the column so that each consumer can see the number at the dispenser's nozzle.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) and their response:

Q: Agar driver k paas cash kam ho tu aap udhaar/credit detay hain?
A: Humari company udhaar nahi deti aur aaj kal k halaat mei tu yeh bilkul mumkin nahi. Hum apko miyaaar/maqdaar aur baki services puri detay hain jo apko kisi aur site pe nahi mila gi. Yahi Taj ki pehchaan hai.

Q: Apki site pe koi Promotion ya scheme chal rhi hai?
A: Humari site pe promotion and scheme har kuch time baad chalti hai jis mei hum apna sathion ko kayi inamaat detay hain. Aur jo humari site pe pehli dafa aa kar fuel karwata hai uska liya gift/inaam bhi hai.

Q: Apki Helpline service humari kis tarah ki help karein ge?
A: Apka jo masla hoga aap Humari helpline pe call kar k bataein ge aur humari kareebi site sa humara numainda aap k paas aa k jo madad kar saka ga kara ga chahay who Accident ka masla ho ya Motorway Police ya Mechanic/Fuel ya koi aur.

Q: Kia aap rastay k liya cash ya iss tarah ki koi aur sahoolat bhi detay hain?
A: Jee humein bilkul ehsaas hai k driver hazraat ko yeh mushkil hoti hai jis ki liya hum yeh sahoolat bilkul detay hain. Humara paas Online, Jazz Cash aur Easy Paisa ki sahulat bhi maujood hai. Aap payment pehla bhi karwa saktay hain aur kisi aur site k zariya bhi karwa saktay hain jis ki madad sa hum driver ko cash ki sahulat detay hain.

Q: Humari gari apka system mei kesa darj ho sakti hai? (Induction process of Vehicle)
A: Aap pehli dafa jab humari site sa fueling karwaein ge, ussi time humara numainda apki gari aur driver ki maloomat le k apki entry humaray system mei kardega. Iska liya humay sirf gari ka number aur company ka naam chahiya hota hai.

CUSTOMER FEEBACK & COMPLAINTS:

Customer complaints can be defined as the expression of customer expectations that have not been met in what business promises in terms of the product or services.
Customer feedback and complaints present a chance for station management and customer relationship improvement. Every day, the station manager should ask for consumer complaints to ensure that all issues are resolved, which will assist to keep customers pleased.
Any customer complaints made at the station to team members or even the station management must be investigated and resolved. Its specifics must be noted on the relevant template (Given Below).
As mentioned previously, customers can call at Taj Ehsas number and register a complaint about station. Solving that complaint is a top priority task. Detail of customer complaint and Station Manager’s action on it will be part of Helpline Report.
