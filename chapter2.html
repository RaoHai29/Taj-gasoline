STATION

SETUP

FUEL RETAIL

Setup for retailing fuels is the heart of the station. Setup can vary station to station due to different sizes and locations of stations. While each station can vary in terms of layout, some standards are mostly common to all stations. This section of the manual highlights such elements of setup.

SIGNAGE TYPES AND

AUXILIARY UNITS

There are different types of signage and auxiliary units. Signage is mainly divided into primary and secondary signage. Lubricant display unit and utility box (full service unit) are key part of auxiliary units.

- Monolith
- Canopy

SECONDARY SIGNAGE

- Building Signs
