EVERY NEXT DESTINATION STARTS WITH A STRONG PARTNERSHIP

We started our journey with you and together we have reached many milestones together. We have delivered our promise of a better experience when it comes to retail fuel stations and we shall continue to aspire to achieve a position where the brand and system of Taj become the hallmark of excellence and the number one preference from our customers and partners.

The operating manual has been designed with a lot of care, encapsulating learnings and best practices learnt from experience in operating highly successful Retail Fuel Stations over more than 50 years.
